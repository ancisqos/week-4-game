Infinity Stones Collector!

How to play the game:


  • Stop Thanos from getting the Infinity Stones for his Infinity Gauntlet! You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four Infinity Stones below. By clicking on a Stone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score found at the bottom of the screen.

  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. Thanos wins and you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

  • The value of each Infinity Stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Stone.
